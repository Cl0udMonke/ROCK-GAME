Deep Core Miner

How to Play

You have 60 seconds to mine as much ore as possible.

  • Tap rocks to destroy them instantly (Small) or deal damage.
  • Common: 1 Tap, 5 Points
  • Uncommon: 2 Taps, 10 Points
  • Rare: 3 Taps, 25 Points (Fast)
  • Legendary: 4 Taps, 50 Points (Slow)

After time ends, buy upgrades to run longer and earn more!
